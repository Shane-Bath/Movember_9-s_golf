MO 9's The Nine hole golf charity event

Movember 9’s is a nine-hole charity golf event in support of Movember to raise awareness and money for testicular cancer. We gather every year in the cold to shoot the lights out in a fast paced nine-hole golf tournament, so we can be back in the club house bar in under two hours, before the onset of hypothermia. Rasing money for testicular cancer should not be a test of one’s endurance, so if you think the nine-holes, shotgun start, better ball format is appropriate for your delicate disposition, sign up today.

This year we will be meeting in Dublin and Galway. Sutton-sur-mer will be hosting the Dublin event at Sutton golf club, a classic seaside links with a well-appointed bar and views. The second event will take place in Galway at the Galway golf club an inland classic with a welcoming club house and a fully stock bar. There will be pro-tournament level of drinks and snacks available on each tee-box, a roving St Bernard style golf cart bar to ensure there is enough libation to protect you against the cold. Live score tracking and prizes for the closes to hole, longest drive and most frequent user of the water hazards. (no speeches) At registration each person will be provided with a goodie bag filled with our lovely sponsors goodies. If all of this sound delightful, for as little as $100 per player you can ensure yourself a spot with the finest of people society has to offer, act now by selecting you club and clicking on the booking link.

Sutton golf club

Establish in 1895, Sutton golf links is a true test of the hardy golfer in you, a true nine-hole classic. A combination of a dry fast fairways and greens, usually accompanied by a refreshing breeze off the Irish sea, this course provides a challenge to wayward golfer but to the ‘down the middle’ amongst us, an opportunity to clean up the accolades and prizes.
