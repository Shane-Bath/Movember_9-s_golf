MO 9's The Nine hole golf charity event

Movember 9’s is a nine-hole charity golf event in support of Movember to raise awareness and money for testicular cancer. We gather every year in the cold to shoot the lights out in a fast paced nine-hole golf tournament, so we can be back in the club house bar in under two hours, before the onset of hypothermia. Rasing money for testicular cancer should not be a test of one’s endurance, so if you think the nine-holes, shotgun start, better ball format is appropriate for your delicate disposition, sign up today.

This year we will be meeting in Dublin and Galway. Sutton-sur-mer will be hosting the Dublin event at Sutton golf club, a classic seaside links with a well-appointed bar and views. The second event will take place in Galway at the Galway golf club an inland classic with a welcoming club house and a fully stock bar. There will be pro-tournament level of drinks and snacks available on each tee-box, a roving St Bernard style golf cart bar to ensure there is enough libation to protect you against the cold. Live score tracking and prizes for the closes to hole, longest drive and most frequent user of the water hazards. (no speeches) At registration each person will be provided with a goodie bag filled with our lovely sponsors goodies.
