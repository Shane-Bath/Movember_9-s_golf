MO 9's - Nine hole golf charity event

Movember 9’s is a nine-hole charity golf event in support of Movember to raise awareness and money for testicular cancer. We gather every year in the cold to shoot the lights out in a fast paced nine-hole golf tournament, so we can be back in the club house bar in under two hours, before the onset of hypothermia. Rasing money for testicular cancer should not be a test of one’s endurance, so if you think the nine-holes, shotgun start, better ball format is appropriate for your delicate disposition, sign up today.
